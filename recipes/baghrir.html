Baghrir



Baghrir are light and spongy Moroccan semolina pancakes. They are usually served warm, soaked in butter and honey.


Ingredients:


  • 250 g fine semolina
  • 50 g flour
  • 1 packet (7 g) dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 600 ml warm water

Instructions:


  1. In a blender, mix the semolina, flour, yeast, baking powder, salt, and warm water.
  2. Blend until the mixture is smooth. Let it rest for 30 minutes at room temperature.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat (no oil needed).
  4. Pour a small ladle of batter into the pan and cook only on one side until bubbles form and the surface is dry.
  5. Do not flip. Remove and repeat with the rest of the batter.
  6. Serve warm with a mixture of melted butter and honey.
