Sfenj



Sfenj is a light and fluffy doughnut, traditionally made in Morocco for breakfast or a snack, it is often enjoyed with honey or sugar.


Ingredients:


  • 500 g (about 4 cups) flour
  • 1 packet (7 g) dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • About 300 ml (1Â¼ cups) warm water
  • Oil for frying

Instructions:


  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, yeast, sugar, and salt.
  2. Gradually add warm water while mixing until you get a sticky dough.
  3. Cover the bowl with a clean cloth and let the dough rest in a warm place for about 1 hour until it doubles in size.
  4. Heat the oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  5. Wet your hands, take a small piece of dough with a hole in the center to form a ring.
  6. Carefully drop the sfenj into the hot oil and fry until golden on both sides.
  7. Drain on paper towels, and you're done !
