Msemen



Msemen are square Moroccan layered pancakes, crispy on the outside and soft inside. Perfect with tea, they can be served plain or with honey and butter.


Ingredients:


  • 300 g flour
  • 200 g fine semolina
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 300ml warm water
  • Oil for folding and cooking
  • Soft butter and extra semolina for layering

Instructions:


  1. Mix flour, semolina, salt, and sugar in a bowl. Gradually add warm water and knead into a smooth, elastic dough.
  2. Divide the dough into small balls, coat lightly in oil, and let rest for 20 minutes.
  3. On an oiled surface, flatten each ball into a thin circle using your hands.
  4. Brush with melted butter, sprinkle with semolina, fold the sides into a square shape.
  5. Cook on a hot pan, flipping until golden and crispy on both sides.
  6. Serve warm, optionally with honey or jam.
